Why Marketing Technology?
We live in a world of all things digital and as a result, marketing and technology are no longer two separate areas of business. Our faces are always in front of screens, and that's exactly where marketing needs to be as well. Because of this digital revolution, a whole new set of skills are needed to build a bridge between the two areas. My skills fall on the technical side of the marketing technology spectrum, specializing in web development in the advertising & marketing realm. But I also have experience with other areas within this world including digital project management, web analytics, search engine optimization, design, and content management.
