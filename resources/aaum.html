Alumni Association of New York City
The University of Michigan Alumni Association in New York City needed to redesign their website to improve usability and overall design. The site is currently being revamped page by page, with uniform components and web friendly content.
Company
Alumni Association of the University of Michigan
Role
  • Web Development
  • Design
Technologies
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Adobe InDesign
The Design
The colors and typography of the site were kept consistent with the parent site - a classic maize and blue theme for the school colors. A big focus of the revamp is diving the pages into smaller, more digestible sections and displaying a concise paragraph when needed. Call-to-actions are bein added throughout the site to guide the user to valuable information.
The Code
The Alumni Association uses HigherLogic Content Manager for the parent site and all microsites. As a result of this setup, the site is coded in pieces used as components, and each page put together within the content manager. A base stylesheet is used across the site and individual stylesheets are added to a page as an additional component.
